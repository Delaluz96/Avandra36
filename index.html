Real-Time Tracking

Real-time tracking in a safety tracking application involves continuously monitoring the location and status of users or assets through GPS technology. It provides instant updates and alerts to ensure immediate response in case of emergencies or safety incidents. This feature enhances situational awareness and enables proactive safety management by allowing supervisors to track movements and receive notifications about potential hazards in real-time.

Alerts and Notifications

Users can receive push notifications, SMS, and email alerts. These features ensure timely communication and immediate response to safety incidents. Customization allows users to set preferences based on the type and severity of events..
